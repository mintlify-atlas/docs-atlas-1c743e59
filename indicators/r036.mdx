---
title: "R036: Lowest bid disqualified"
description: "Detects when the lowest submitted bid is disqualified while the award criterion is price only"
---

The lowest submitted bid is disqualified, while the award criterion is price only.

## Methodology

A contracting process is flagged if:

- There are one or more active awards.
- There are one or more valid bids with amounts.
- The lowest submitted bid is disqualified.

<Tip>
**Example**

The Public Works Department sets the award criterion as lowest price. FairDeal Inc. submits a bid of $100,000. BribeCorp submits a bid of $111,111. The Public Works Department disqualifies the bid from FairDeal Inc.
</Tip>

<Info>
**Why is this a red flag?**

A corrupt buyer can award the pre-determined bidder by disqualifying lower-priced bids.
</Info>

<sub>Based on "The lowest bid is rejected almost inexplicably" in [*Fraud in Public Procurement: A collection of Red Flags and Best Practices*](https://ec.europa.eu/sfc/system/files/documents/sfc-files/fraud-public-procurement-final-20122017-ares20176254403.pdf), "Lowest bidder not selected" in [*Common Red Flags of Fraud and Corruption in Procurement*](https://web.archive.org/web/20200918183744/https://www.worldbank.org/en/about/unit/integrity-vice-presidency/brief/common-red-flags-of-fraud-and-corruption-in-procurement), and "Award to other than lowest qualified bidder" in [*Guide to Combating Corruption & Fraud in Infrastructure Development Projects*](https://guide.iacrc.org/red-flag-award-to-other-than-lowest-qualified-bidder/).</sub>

## Output

The indicator's value is always 1.0.

## Configuration

The indicator is not configurable at the indicator level.

<Note>
See global settings for `no_price_comparison_procurement_methods` and `price_comparison_procurement_methods` to control when price comparison indicators are applied.
</Note>

## Implementation Details

The indicator (from `src/indicators/r036.rs`):

- Only applies to procurement methods where price comparison is relevant
- Tracks the lowest bid amount across all submitted bids
- Handles currency matching (skips bids with mismatched currencies)
- Checks if the lowest bid has status "disqualified"
- Requires at least one valid bid with an amount to be present
- Flags the process if the lowest bid is disqualified and there are valid bids

<Warning>
Bids with different currencies than the first observed currency are skipped. The tool logs warnings about currency mismatches (can be configured to info level).
</Warning>

## Exclusions

A contracting process is excluded if:

- Prices are predetermined by the buyer (based on procurement method configuration).
